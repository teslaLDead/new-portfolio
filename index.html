MereExams

UI/UX Design

Website redesign for an education consultancy firm called MereExam. MereExams provided information about colleges, exams and careers. The site required complete redesign with respect to both UX and UI.

About MereExams

MereExams is an online educational portal for higher education in India enabling the students to get the right information on time thus saving their time and energy to let them concentrate on just studying.



Work Required

MereExams required a complete websit redisgn that delivered the vast information in an organized way.
A new color theme was required so make an interactive UI.
Responsive and interactive webdesign.

Preparing for The Process

I started preparing for the redesign by analyzing the website and going through their original workflow. Analyzed what kind of information was being shown and how their different stakeholders acceced their information.

Then I started looking at their competitions to check out what is working for them and what is not.
